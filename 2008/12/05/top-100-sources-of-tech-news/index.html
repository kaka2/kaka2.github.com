top 100 Sources Of Tech News

12/5/08

對于一個關注國外it（互聯網、科技）資訊的網迷來說，Techmeme.com無疑是個人rss reader的首選訂閱源之一了。相比于digg.com這種互動，techmeme的內容則來自于各個blog或非blog站點上的信息聚合。從05年～08年之間得到過很多的稱贊和好評。

“Techmeme, the best aggregator of technology news！” — Nick Denton, Founder and Publisher, Gawker Media (6/5/07)

“Like Scoble, I find it's changed the way I think about blogging. Not many of these tech gadgets do that.” — Dave Winer, personal media pioneer (9/16/05)
