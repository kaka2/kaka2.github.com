Dear ThinkFree Customer,
Have you heard about ThinkFree's New Online Service?
Starting April 24 we are going to provide you with a whole new experience.
Unfortunately, that means we have to take down the current site to make the necessary changes. ThinkFree Online will be temporarily unavailable from 23rd, 6:00 PM to 24th, 11:00 AM PDT on April.
